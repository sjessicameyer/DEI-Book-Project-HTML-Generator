"Julian is a Mermaid" by Jessica Love

"This book is about a little kid who wants to be a mermaid and dress up, it shows kids they can express themselves however they want and it’s more than okay to be themselves. It’s not specifically about any identity or anything, just it’s okay to be you :) It has amazing art and is just a sweet book." - LHS Sophomore

"George" by Alex Gino

"I think kids should be exposed to more diverse literature at an earlier age, and this book is an LGBTQ+ book written by an LGBTQ+ author." - Clarke 7th

"The Year of the Rat" by Grace Lin

"I loved this book because I was able to see myself in her shoes when I was little. This was the first book that I read in elementary school that mirrored by life experience up until that point. I carried it around with me wherever I went because she narrated the story so beautifully and it was her true life experience- the ups, the downs, the real moments." - LHS Senior
